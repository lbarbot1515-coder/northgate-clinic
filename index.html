About Us

Built on trust,
driven by excellence

Founded in 1989 by Dr. Margaret H. Caldwell and Dr. Elias M. Osei, Northgate Wellness & Medical Center has grown from a two-physician family practice into one of the most comprehensive private medical centers in Contra Costa County, California.

Today, NWMC operates across three campuses, employs over 340 staff members, and serves more than 48,000 registered patients annually. We hold affiliations with St. Mary's Regional Hospital and the UCSF Health Network.
